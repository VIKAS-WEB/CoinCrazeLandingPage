Delete Your CoinCraze Account

We respect your right to privacy and allow you to request deletion of your CoinCraze account and associated personal data at any time.

How to Request Account Deletion

  • Send an email to our support team at: support@coincraze.app
  • Include the subject line: “Account Deletion Request”
  • Include the registered email or phone number used to create your CoinCraze account.

What Happens Next?

  • We will verify your identity using the provided contact details.
  • All associated data including your KYC documents, wallet info, and transaction history will be deleted within 7 working days.
  • If certain data must be retained for legal or regulatory reasons (such as tax laws), we will notify you accordingly.

Need Help?

You can contact our support team at support@coincraze.app for any questions or clarifications.
